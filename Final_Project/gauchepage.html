About Gauche

Gauche is a member of the Black Bulls and is known for his mirror magic and his obsession with his sister, Marie. He has a strong sense of loyalty to his friends and is a skilled magic knight.

Gauche's Magic

Gauche wields Mirror Magic, which allows him to create and manipulate mirrors. He can use his magic to reflect attacks and create illusions.
