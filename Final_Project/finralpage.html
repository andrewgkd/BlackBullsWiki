About Finral

Finral is a member of the Black Bulls and is known for his charm and flirtatious personality. He has a strong sense of loyalty to his friends and is a skilled magic knight.

Finral's Magic

Finral wields Spatial Magic, which allows him to create portals and manipulate space. This makes him a valuable asset in battles and missions.
