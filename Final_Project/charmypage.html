About Charmy

Charmy is a member of the Black Bulls and is known for her adorable appearance and love for food. She has a strong sense of loyalty to her friends and is a skilled magic knight.

Charmy's Magic

Charmy wields Cotton Magic, which allows her to create and manipulate cotton. She can also use her magic to create food and has a unique ability to transform into a powerful form.
