About Henry

Henry is a member of the Black Bulls and is known for his charming personality and powerful blood magic. He has a strong sense of loyalty to his friends and is a skilled magic knight.

Henry's Magic

Henry wields Blood Magic, which allows him to manipulate his own blood and the blood of others. This makes him a formidable opponent in battle.
