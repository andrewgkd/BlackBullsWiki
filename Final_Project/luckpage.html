About Luck

Luck is a member of the Black Bulls and is known for his cheerful personality and love for battle. He has a strong sense of loyalty to his friends and is a skilled magic knight.

Luck's Magic

Luck wields Lightning Magic, which allows him to manipulate lightning and create powerful attacks. He is known for his speed and agility in battle.
