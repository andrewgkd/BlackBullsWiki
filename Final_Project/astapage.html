About Asta

Asta is a member of the Black Bulls and the main protagonist of the series. He is known for his unwavering determination, hard work, and his dream of becoming the Wizard King. Despite being born without magic, he compensates with his physical strength and anti-magic abilities.

Asta's Magic

Asta wields a unique form of magic known as Anti-Magic, which allows him to nullify other magic spells. This makes him a formidable opponent against magic users.
